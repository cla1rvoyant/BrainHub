Rhythm

In nature, everything is subject to rhythm. The human body is designed in such a way that it adopts the rhythms dictated by the external environment. Musical rhythm can influence the rhythm of heartbeats, adjust the rhythm of breathing. If a person is imposed on a rhythm unusual for him, he will feel discomfort, irritation and aggression, and repeated exposure can lead to diseases. At a rhythm multiple of 1.5 beats / sec, accompanied by powerful super frequencies (15-30 Hz), a person experiences ecstasy. At a rhythm of 2 beats / sec, at the same frequencies, it enters into a narcotic state. And if you imagine that such music sounds for several hours without a break, the rhythm is pumped up, the signals go to the brain of the audience at the concert (and this is, mainly, young people 13-20 years old), then the most harmless thing that music lovers can expect is a temporary loss orientation in space and sensations similar to an alcoholic hangover.

Frequency

The human ear perceives the range from 15-16 Hz to 20,000-22,000 Hz. Above - ultrasound, below - infrasound. The human ear does not perceive it, but there is an effect on the body. Uncontrolled exposure to ultrasound is dangerous: damage to internal organs, hemorrhages, edema, inflammation, arthritis occurs. In the brain, biochemical reactions occur, similar to the injection of morphine. What we need to know to be healthy

1. Scientific research.
Psychologists, together with neurologists, performed the following experiment. Candidates were selected and brain activity recorded before and after listening to contemporary popular music. In addition, memorization tests were used, asking to memorize 20 words. While listening to collections of popular songs, a sharp decrease in the number of hotspots in the right hemisphere and a slight decrease in hotspots in the left were quite noticeable. The memorization test after listening to music was much more difficult than before listening.
The subjects returned to their normal state only after an hour.

2. The impact of rock music on humans.
Not only popular music negatively affects a person, it is also hard rock. Here, too, rhythm, frequency of alternating light, volume are used. An overabundance of high or low frequencies seriously injures the brain. American medical scientist David Elkin proved that a high-volume shrill sound contributes to protein folding.

3. The influence of music on plants.
These experiments have been carried out more than once. The same seeds were taken, planted in the same conditions, in a word, everything that could affect their growth was observed. The first group of seeds was placed under a 3 hour exposure to hard rock. The second group of seeds was pop music at the same time. The third group of seeds to the classics, Bach's music for 3 hours. After a certain time, when the seeds sprouted and grew, it turned out that the smallest plants are in the first group, then in the second and the tallest and healthiest in the third group. Similar experiments were carried out more than once, and on other plants, and the fruits of the plants were evaluated.
